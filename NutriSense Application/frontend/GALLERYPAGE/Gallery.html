What We
Do
Using advanced computer vision techniques, NutriSense detects visible signs of nutrient deficiencies, such as discoloration and deformities, and classifies them accordingly. Whether it's nitrogen, potassium, or phosphorus, NutriSense knows what your plant is missing.
NutriSense.com
AI meets
agriculture
NutriSense is an AI-powered plant health monitoring system designed to support farmers and agriculturists. By analyzing leaf images, NutriSense identifies plant species, detects nutrient deficiencies, assesses severity, and recommends targeted fertilizers.
NutriSense.com
PERSONALIZED
FERTILIZERS IT IS!
Based on the type and severity of deficiency, NutriSense provides personalized fertilizer recommendations. This smart feedback loop helps farmers take immediate, informed action to protect and improve crop yields.
