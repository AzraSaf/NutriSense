Why us

Turning leaf images into
solutions!

By analyzing leaf images, NutriSense identifies plant species, detects nutrient deficiencies, assesses severity, and recommends targeted fertilizers.


Leaf Species Identification
Identifies the plant species (banana, rice, or coffee)
using image classification techniques. This ensures accurate analysis and species-specific treatment recommendations.

Plant Deficiency Identification along with Severity
Analyzes the uploaded leaf image to detect specific nutrient deficiencies. It also assesses the severity level to guide proper urgency and response.

Personalized Fertilizer Recommendation
Provides tailored fertilizer suggestions based on plant type, deficiency, and soil. Recommends optimal dosage and method to enhance plant recovery and growth.
