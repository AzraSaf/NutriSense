About NutriSense

Welcome to NutriSense! Our goal is to help farmers and gardeners detect plant nutrient deficiencies using AI-powered image analysis.

Simply upload a photo of your plant leaves, and our system will analyze and provide recommendations to improve plant health.

We are passionate about sustainable agriculture and empowering users with innovative technology.
